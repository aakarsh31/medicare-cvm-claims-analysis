# Medicare CVM Claims Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of Medicare CCLF (Claims and Claims Line Feed) data to generate actionable insights for a pharmaceutical company's Cardiovascular Metabolic (CVM) Sales & Marketing leadership team.

## Business Context
CVM diseases — including heart disease, stroke, and type 2 diabetes — represent a growing share of healthcare claims. This project analyzes 2016-2018 Medicare claims data to answer key business questions around CVM trends, HCP segmentation, and patient demographics.

## Key Business Questions Addressed
1. **CVM Claims Trend** — What is the share of CVM claims as a percentage of total claims, and how is it changing?
2. **HCP Segmentation** — How should the sales force be deployed across different provider segments?
3. **Patient Demographics** — How do patient age groups influence marketing budget allocation?
4. **Gender Analysis** — How do CVM claims differ by patient gender?
5. **Procedure Breakdown** — Which CVM procedures are driving volume, and how should products be positioned?

## Key Findings
- CVM claims grew 35.4% (2016-2018), outpacing overall claims growth of 28.8%
- 16.4% of claims had null provider NPIs — flagged as a data quality concern
- The 70-79 age group overtook 60-69 as the largest CVM segment by 2018
- Male CVM claims are growing at 43.6% vs 29.8% for females, narrowing the gender gap
